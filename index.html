The Photographer

Passion for Authentic Moments

Hello, I'm Chintu Pathak. I believe that photography is far more than just taking pictures — it is the art of pausing time. My passion lies in documenting life's most genuine, unscripted moments so that they can be cherished for a lifetime.

From the grandeur of a wedding celebration to the subtle, quiet confidence found in a portrait session, I strive to capture the true essence of my subjects. With an eye for dramatic lighting and a commitment to high-end editing, my goal is to frame your reality beautifully and cinematically.
